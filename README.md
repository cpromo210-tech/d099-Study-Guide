# WGU D099 Sales Management Study Kit

Two browser-based study tools that got me through D099. Both are single HTML files. Download and open in any browser, or use the live links below. Nothing to install, works offline.

## Live links

- Study guide: https://cpromo210-tech.github.io/d099-Study-Guide/d099-study-guide.html
- Adaptive drill: https://cpromo210-tech.github.io/d099-Study-Guide/d099-adaptive-drill.html

## What's inside

**Study guide** — Flashcard reference covering all 13 sections, 257 cards. Searchable, tap a question to reveal the answer. Includes an ELI5 power-card section for the highest-yield concepts.

**Adaptive drill** — 350 questions filterable by source: the 70-question PA, the unit tests, the module quizzes (with full case-study scenarios attached), and a Focus tab for the heaviest-weighted concepts. Tracks your accuracy per topic, remembers across sessions, and feeds you more of whatever you keep missing. Every question is tagged with its origin and explains why the right answer is right.

## How the answers were sourced

Questions came from the module quizzes, unit tests, and the PA. Answer keys were verified against the official WGU study guide, multiple student study guides, and cross-referenced Quizlet decks, comparing keys across sources rather than trusting any single one. For trickier concepts, the answers were checked against textbook material.

Note: WGU does not publish official keys for the unit and module quizzes, so treat these as well-verified rather than gospel. Your own graded feedback always wins.
